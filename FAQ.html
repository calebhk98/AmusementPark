Outside food and drinks are not permitted inside the park, but we offer a variety of dining options to cater to different dietary preferences. However, guests with specific dietary needs may bring their own food, subject to park guidelines.

Yes, lockers can be rented near the park entrance for a small fee of $5 for small lockers and $8 for large lockers. They are available on a first-come, first-served basis.

Adventure Land offers group discounts for parties of 15 or more, as well as special packages for birthdays, school trips, and corporate events. Please visit the Group Events section on our website for more information.

We recommend wearing comfortable clothing and shoes, as you'll be walking and exploring throughout the day. Keep in mind that some attractions may involve water, so it's advisable to pack a change of clothes or a poncho if needed.

Yes, any lost items or found items can be reported at our guest services desk near the park entrance. While we do our best to reunite lost items with their owners, Adventure Land is not responsible for lost or stolen items.

Yes, you may leave and re-enter the park on the same day. Just make sure to keep your admission ticket or wristband for re-entry.

Pets are not permitted inside the park, with the exception of service animals for guests with disabilities. Please ensure your service animal is properly identified and leashed at all times.

Adventure Land is conveniently located at 1234 Adventure Lane, easily accessible by car and public transportation. If driving, take exit 6 off Highway Adventure. For public transit, you can take the Adventure Bus line to the Adventure Stop. Detailed directions and parking information are available in the Location & Parking section on our website.

Adventure Land offers various ticketing options, including single-day and multi-day passes, as well as season pass memberships. Adult single-day tickets are priced at $45, while children (aged 3-12) and senior citizens (aged 65+) tickets are priced at $35. Most attractions and rides are included with park admission, but some premium experiences may require an additional fee. See our Ticket Pricing & Information section for more details.

Adventure Land offers a diverse range of rides and attractions designed to cater to all age groups and interests. These include roller coasters like the Thundering Titan, water rides such as Splash Mountain, interactive play areas, scenic tours on the Adventure Express, live shows, and more. Explore our Attractions & Rides section for a full list of offerings.
